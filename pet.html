Brief Introduction

My favourite pet is a dog. Dogs are considered a distinct species in the wolf family.They are also considered to be Man's best friend. They are passived to be one of the most intelligent animals.

Dogs Roles

Dogs have different roles in our society. These roles can be as follows:

  • Dogs are kept as pets
  • Dogs can be kept as workers i.e taking care of livestock(herding)
  • Dogs help people wth special health conditions as their companions
  • Dogs are kept for providing security to their owners
  • In some parts of the world like Asia, dogs are bred for food.
